Podcast episode

01 December 2024

Check out the interview here! I had an honor to be interviewed by Florian Helinski, for his Atomen Enzo podcast. Florian’s goal is to interview researchers in Utrecht University about their research in a way accessible to the general public. Hence, we talked about my research, but the light approach of Florian to ask questions, made me feel we were talking about life in general. It was an amazing idea not to recieve the questions in advance - this way I felt more relaxed and the discussion flowed naturally. He challenged me to explain in plain words what I do in my research, not sure I succeeded, but I did my best. I very much enjoyed the interview and meeting Florian!
