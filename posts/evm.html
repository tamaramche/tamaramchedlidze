European Values Maps

20 December 2022

Check out the maps here! European Value Maps are conceptual maps of human values across Europe. They allow public to reflect on the sheer variety of opinions that are held by European citizens. The maps are created using European Values Study data set. Enjoy exploring the maps, and try the “Show me on the map” feature! Please do not hesitate to get back to the team with any suggestions/comments! There is a number of open problems stemming from these visualization, which include but are not limited to:

  • how does the general public read and interpret the maps
  • how to construct more readable maps
  • how to generalize these visualization techniques to time-dependent data surveys
