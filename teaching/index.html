Computer Graphics

  • In collaboration with Peter Vangorp - 4th period 2025 webpage

This is an introductory course to computer graphics. Computer graphics deals with the algorithmic processing of visual (images) and spatial (geometry) data. The course focuses on the very basics of modeling and rendering, i.e., the mathematical description of three-dimensional scenes and how to create (realistic) images of such models.
